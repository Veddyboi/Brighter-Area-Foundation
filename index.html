About Us

The World's Best Video Game Hosting Tournament Foundation That You Can Trust.

We are a group of young individuals working to make the world a better place by holding video game tournaments and raising money for the underprivileged in the Bay Area.

A little about us Ved Desai, Kamal Kandi, and Ajay Chandran are the founding members of this group. In the Bay Area, particularly in South San Francisco, we are attempting to create activities to raise money. We'll buy food and supplies to give them with the money we receive, and we'll save some of the rest for future events.
